Frequently asked questions

Everything you need
to know

What exactly does Nexora Ai do?+
We build and deploy custom AI automation systems for SMEs — AI agents, lead generation, social media automation, business process automation, and custom AI tools, all tailored to your specific industry.
How long does it take to get started?+
Most clients have their first automation live within 7–14 days. We start with a free 30-minute discovery call, design a blueprint, build, then deploy directly into your existing tools — zero disruption.
Do I need any technical knowledge?+
Absolutely not. We handle everything — design, development, integration, and training. You'll have a dedicated account manager who explains everything in plain English. If you can use email, you can use our systems.
How much does it cost?+
Flexible monthly retainers start from £997/month depending on scope. Every engagement begins with a free discovery call and a clear written quote — no hidden fees, no surprises. Most clients see positive ROI within the first 30 days.
Will AI replace my team?+
No — AI augments your team, it doesn't replace them. Our goal is to eliminate the repetitive, low-value tasks that drain your team's energy, so they can focus on high-value work like building relationships, closing deals, and delivering exceptional service.
Is my data safe and GDPR compliant?+
Yes. We build all solutions with data privacy as a core principle. We're GDPR-compliant, use enterprise-grade encryption, and never share your data with third parties. Industry-specific compliance (e.g. HIPAA for healthcare) is built in where required.
What tools and platforms do you integrate with?+
We integrate with most major platforms: HubSpot, Salesforce, Zoho, Google Workspace, Microsoft 365, Calendly, Instagram, LinkedIn, TikTok, WhatsApp, Mailchimp, Shopify, WordPress, and many more. Ask us about your specific stack.
What support do you provide after launch?+
All clients receive ongoing support, monitoring, and optimisation. We provide monthly performance reports, proactively suggest improvements, and your dedicated account manager is available via Slack or email — typically responding within a few hours.
